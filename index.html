§ Who We Are

An Ancient Order, Reborn

The Organisation of the Sacred Snail has gathered since time immemorial — or at least since last Tuesday — to venerate the humble yet magnificent gastropod. We believe in the snail's wisdom: slow deliberation, carrying your home within yourself, leaving a shimmering trail wherever you go.

This website is under active, loving construction. We are working tirelessly (at a snail's pace, naturally) to bring you the full glory of snail-kind.

§ Our Sacred Creed

The Principles of the Shell

🐌

Slow is Holy

We reject the tyranny of speed. The snail takes its time, and the snail always arrives.

🏠

Home is Sacred

Your shell is yours. Carry it proudly. No force in this world can separate you from it.

✨

Leave a Trail

Wherever you go, leave something shimmering behind. A life unlived is a dry rock.

🌧️

Thrive in the Rain

While others hide from storms, the snail emerges. Hardship is our element.
